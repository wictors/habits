7 Habits of Highly Effective Programmers

1. Exercises, Sport, Gym, other physical activity. Good sit position. Frequent breaks, try do not dry your eyes from monitors/displays. 
2. Need to do get project out the door.  Keep your eye, mind on the goal, Don't waste large periods of time on small bugs.
3. Write simple code, simple solution. Understandable and consistency for others. Don't over-engineer. 
4. To have uninterrupted hours without distructions. Just sit down and conding, focus on problem all the time. 
5. Always be learning. Get out of comfort zone.
6. Collaboration. It is not a solo activity. Programing is like team sport with your colleagues. Others may have some solutions for my problems.
